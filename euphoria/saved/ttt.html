About the series

The plot of the series tells the story of a group of high school

students who learn the world through drugs, sex, injuries, social

networks, love and friendship.

The Euphoria of HBO is an American adaptation of the Israeli

show of the same name.

In the original version, the main characters were:

a teenager who gets involved in the world of pornography;

a young gay who finds shelter in the home of a much older

couple; illicit substance abuser; amateur chemist who creates

new drugs and supplies them to schoolchildren.

It is not clear exactly how much the American version will match the original.

It is only known that the plot will be focused on the heroine.

Episods

Teenager Rue Bennett returns home from rehab, and immediately

buys more drugs from her friend Fezco.

Jules is a new girl in town taking summer school.

Her classmate Kat invites her to a house party hosted by a popular

college football player, Chris McKay.

Jules initially declines, opting instead to meet up with an older

man from a hookup app.

Rue gets home late one night and her mother forces her to take a

drug test. In order to pass it, Rue acquires clean urine by asking her

childhood friend Lexi for some of hers. Lexi's sister Cassie is dating

McKay, who discusses Cassie's sexual past with his friend Nate.

Meanwhile, Jules almost attacks Nate after he continually harasses

her and Kat loses her virginity. Upon leaving, Jules runs into Rue,

who goes home with her.

As Nate returns home from the party, it is revealed that Jules' hookup was Nate's father, Cal.
